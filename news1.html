Ubisoft Announces New Assassin’s Creed

Published: July 2026

Ubisoft has officially confirmed that a new Assassin’s Creed title is currently under development. The company stated that the upcoming entry aims to expand the franchise’s scope while maintaining its historical-action identity.

The announcement comes amid ongoing shifts in Ubisoft’s development strategy, with increased focus on long-term franchise support and live-service integration. More details are expected in the coming months.

Why it matters: Assassin’s Creed remains one of Ubisoft’s most valuable franchises. A new entry could signal strategic recalibration after recent financial pressures and mixed reception of previous releases.
